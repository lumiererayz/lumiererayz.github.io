Child Development Initiatives Nigeria

Welcome to child development initiatives Nigeria (CDIN) we are a nonprofit organization in the social, mental and educational development of children and young adults growing up in difficult circumstances and the society in general. CDIN believes in the strength of a child, both male and female, supporting their growth and also addressing major causes of the problem they encounter. We invite you to help influence and contribute to the right culture in our society.

BOY-CHILD AND LOVE

LOVE FOR THE BOY-CHILD

The program tagged Boy child and love' is design to enlighten, rehabilitate and mentor young and vibrant male students across secondary schools on self discovery, social morals, sexual health and Career development. CDI aim to improve practices and societal orientation of young make child (8-18) years through our training, advocacy and mentorship program so as to reduce the increasing rate of social vices in our community.Kick off date-21st Feb.

Let’s do it together

To realize our dream of ‘a happy life for all children’ we want and need to work together with others who share our passion and professionalism. CDIN is always looking to build new partnerships.

Partners

At CDIN we believe in the power of cooperation. In partnership with other organizations we develop and implement projects that promote children’s well-being. Are you a local or international child rights organization? CDIN can support you through training, research and advice.

Donors

CDIN is a vibrant organization, constantly developing innovative initiatives that improve the well-being of children growing up in difficult circumstances. We need your support to turn our ideas into reality, scaling them up and making a difference.
