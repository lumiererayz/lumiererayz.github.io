A happy life for all children

We are a nonprofit organization in the field of social science and public health. We aim to improve the social, mental and educational development of children and young adults growing up in difficult circumstances and the society at large. CDIN believes in the strength of children and young persons, supporting their growth and addressing major causes of the problem the face. We aim to improve policies, practices and societal orientations, by building the skills of children, parents, communities, organizations and authorities through our programs. Our dream of changing the narratives of the African child motivates and drives us to do more for our communities

Our Team

We are a small but dedicated group of professionals with academic background in social sciences and public health. Our organization consists of people with extensive experience in child and youth related issues.
