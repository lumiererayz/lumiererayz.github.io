SPOTLIGHT ON: BOY CHILD AND LOVE

21ST Febuary, 2019

On the 21st of February, child development initiatives Nigeria set on course her first project on boy child, tagged ‘boychild n love’ which is aim at educating them on values and that improves their mentality and knowledge positively. At exactly 1: 30pm on Thursday 21st the ‘boychild and love project kicks off at Ataoja school of science main auditorium. In attendance was about 120 male student of the school from across all classes and levels of the high school. Our team members in person of Mr Olaitan, mr Abolaji, mr Moyinoluwa, miss Jumoke and miss Adeola. With the permission of the school principal, we start the program with a prayer session initiated by one of the student. Mr olaitan the project team head start the program by first introducing CDIN as a organization an here mission and vision for children and adolescents he further went on to talk on why boychild has top understand their selves and grow within the knowledge of who they are and where they belong, he explains to the boys to encourage self discovery and personal development so that they can be their own man and not just be influence by just anything. Mr. moyinoluwa then took over the podium to discuss knowing your friend as a topic, a directive message on how a boychild Can chose his friends and whom they associates with, he also went ahead to discuss sexual health and sexual consciousness with them in a bid to educate them on how to manage their sexual tendencies and relate with opposite sex a better way.
