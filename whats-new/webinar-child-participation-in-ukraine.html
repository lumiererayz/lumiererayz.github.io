Webinar: Child Participation in Ukraine!

April 26, 2016

Community Reintegration and Psychosocial support of internally displaced children in Kyiv and Odesa oblast

Today Remi Goossens, Mathijs Euwema and Rutger van Oudenhoven gave a webinar on child participation, its importance and the introduction of practical tools like the “Youth News Team”. These tools will be used in Youth Camps that host local youth as well as those displaced by the conflict in Ukraine. Besides having fun, we hope to build the resilience of these children and help their integration into their new homes.
