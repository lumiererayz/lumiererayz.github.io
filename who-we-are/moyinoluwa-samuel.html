MOYINOLUWA SAMUEL

Program Director

Moyinoluwa Samuel also known as Moe chuks is a Senior Marketing Manager at ScreenHut Concepts Inc., a photography and branding organization with its major focus on phenomenal/unique image creation, natural beautification and cultural development. The strongest forte of the firm he manages is: showcasing artistic creativity vis-à-vis promoting love/appreciation for arts and natural beauty in the society. With ScreenHut Team, he has consulted on policy formulation, brand development and fashion/trend improvement with top designers and beauty artists in the country and has a wealth of experience in the fashion industry. He has also consulted on communication campaigns at several levels. Moe is a Life Coach, a promoter of culinary wonders (international and local) and business consultant with relevant trainings. He has undergone several trainings in photography, marketing, human resource management, content branding and more. Mr moe is currently the program director at Child Development Initiative Nigeria (CDIN), He also works as the strategic director of the Achievers Consortium University of Osun which all Aline with his commitment and desire to contribute to social and educational development of adolescents and youth in the societyis a Senior Marketing Manager at ScreenHut Concepts Inc., a photography and branding organization with its major focus on phenomenal/unique image creation, natural beautification and cultural development.
