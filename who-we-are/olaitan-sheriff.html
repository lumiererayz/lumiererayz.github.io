Olaitan Sheriff Abiodun

Director

Olaitan Sheriff Abiodun is the convener of child development initiatives Nigeria, a public health practitioner, a researcher and a professional data analyst. Member, The network towards unity for health. Member, Nigerian association of public health professionals and pioneer president Nigerian association of public health students. He attended the osun state university between the year 2014 and 2018, dye to his excellent track record, he was elected the departmental president at the department of community medicine, college of health sciences UNIOSUN and latter became the national president. Olaitan Sheriff has work in various humanitarian capacity locally and nation wide as a researcher, youth advocator and social worker. His impact in child health development, particularly girlchild health advocacy and boychild moral development are part of his success story as a public health expert. As a youth peer educator and social worker, he has reach out to more than 1000 adolescent and young adult in southwest Nigeria, sharing his believes and knowledge in social development. He is a enterprising young entrepreneur and writer with exemplary leadership and management skills
